Privacy: this page has no backend and no storage — every number lives only in your browser tab for the current session. Nothing is sent anywhere or saved anywhere. Closing the tab or refreshing erases it.

How this is calculated: each month, essential + discretionary expenses (plus any irregular items due that month) are subtracted from income. If the result is negative and essentials aren't fully covered by income alone, the shortfall is drawn from the emergency fund. If the fund can't cover it, that month is flagged and counted toward the funding gap below. Surplus months contribute your set amount to the fund (skipped in shortfall months). This is a planning estimate, not financial advice — treat the "extra income needed" figure as a rough target.
